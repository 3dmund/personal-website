About Me

Hey, what's up, hello

I'm a rising sophmore and intended (almost declared) computer science major at UC Berkeley. I am passionate about computer science, and I find it amazing what one can do with it. What's even more amazing is technology's growing importance in today's society; my lifelong dream is to make my mark on this technological revolution. I hope to do this through working a software engineering job immediately after graduating and eventually starting my own company.

I just finished taking my favorite class ever, CS61B, a class on data structures. I loved this class because it introduced me to actual engineering aspect of computer science. I learned so much about modularizing, utilizing data structures, and writing clean code through the various class projects. I enjoyed them so much, that ever since, I have been looking for ways to build my own projects outside the classroom. Recently, I have gotten into web design/development, having created a dance website for my mom and started working on this, my personal website. I am also looking forward to begin working on a startup-worthy idea with a couple of my friends when I return to Berkeley.

My interests extend way beyond technology. I love opportunities to become one with nature, such as hiking along some of the beautiful trails around the bay area. I also consider myself somewhat of an athlete, having played ice hockey at travel and high school levels throughout most of my youth.
